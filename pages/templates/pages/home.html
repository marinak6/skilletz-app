Skilletz

Connecting students to the people they need

Problem

Who hasn't needed to find study partners and groups throughout the college grind? Whether you are looking for group mates for semester long projects, want help from students that have already taken a class, or just need to cram for that exam coming up, we have you covered.

We at Skilletz think that finding peers is too hard, and it really shouldn't be. We should be able to find peers based on major, classes, or experience without any trouble. We hope to make the spread of information easier, and to help all students learn in the environment that suits them best.

Getting Started

With Skilletz, connecting with your peers is as easy as one, two, three.

  1. Login with your UVA email account with the button in the top right corner
  2. Create your profile with basic information used to find student matches
  3. Explore! Flesh out your profile, search for peers, and enjoy.
